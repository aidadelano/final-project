City Island Lobster House is an absolute gem in The Bronx, and a must-visit for seafood lovers! From the moment you walk in, the warm and inviting atmosphere makes you feel right at home. The staff is incredibly friendly, attentive, and knowledgeable, ensuring a seamless dining experience. The menu is a seafood lover's dream—so many fresh and delicious options to choose from! I tried the lobster tail and the seafood platter, and both were cooked to perfection. The flavors were rich, the portions generous, and everything tasted as though it had just been caught that morning. Their garlic butter sauce is divine, and it pairs beautifully with the shellfish. The location on City Island is another highlight. The waterfront views add an extra layer of charm, making it perfect for a romantic dinner or a family outing. The restaurant is spacious, yet cozy, and even when busy, the service remains top-notch. City Island Lobster House is more than just a meal—it's an experience. I can't recommend it enough and am already planning my next visit. If you're craving exceptional seafood and a memorable dining atmosphere, this is the place to be!
Nestled in the heart of The Bronx's Little Italy, Enzo’s is the epitome of authentic Italian dining. The cozy, old-world charm of the restaurant immediately sets the tone for a warm and welcoming experience. Their menu is a masterpiece of traditional Italian dishes, and everything is prepared with care and precision. I highly recommend the veal parmigiana—perfectly tender, with a rich tomato sauce and just the right amount of cheese. The handmade pastas, especially the gnocchi Bolognese, are melt-in-your-mouth delicious. Enzo’s is perfect for date nights or family dinners, and their exceptional service makes you feel like part of the family. It’s truly a culinary treasure on Arthur Avenue.
If you’re in the mood for fresh seafood with bold flavors and generous portions, Sammy’s Fish Box on City Island is the place to be. Their menu is extensive, offering everything from jumbo shrimp to buttery lobster tails. The seafood paella is a standout dish, packed with perfectly cooked shellfish and vibrant flavors. The service is quick and friendly, and the festive atmosphere makes every visit feel like a celebration. The complimentary cornbread and crackers are a nice touch, and the waterfront location adds to the charm. Sammy’s is a fantastic spot for seafood lovers and anyone looking for a lively dining experience. Whether you’re craving Italian classics or fresh seafood, Enzo’s and Sammy’s are two Bronx institutions that never disappoint. Both offer impeccable flavors, warm hospitality, and a true taste of the borough's diverse culinary excellence.
